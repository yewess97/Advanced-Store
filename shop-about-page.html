Summary history about our store

Advanced Store is a corporation founded and entirely owned by Yousif and his team. The store was founded in 2015 and has received no outside investments. "Advanced" sells three different clothes products that command impressive market share. Yousif is interested in taking the store to the next level in sales by 2024.

The Growth of our store

"Advanced" is participating in a $3.8 billion market that will have a 20% growth rate for the next three years. "Advanced" will be targeting three customer segments: Men, Women, and Children. These markets are growing at 5%, 8%, and 10% respectively. These markets have 20,000, 30,000, and 15,000 potential customers respectively. These figures are the USA market. The major trend in the market is toward international sales.

Our Products

Each pair is made of 100 percent cotton for a comfortable, breathable fit regardless of the weather. "Advanced" offers a wide range of apparel to fit any men's, woman’s and kid's unique sense of style. Our clothing and accessories are carefully curated to provide our customers the latest fashions. To keep our customers in style we post new arrivals daily, as well as offer stylist picks to help any indecisive shoppers. We've got all the stuff you need to invent a style that’s all yours.

Our Strategy

"Advanced" will follow four concise strategies to achieve the desired growth. We will build customized versions of our standard products, providing more value for specific groups of customers. We will develop a strong marketing infrastructure. This will be key for us to get our products out on the market. Lastly, "Advanced" will focus on follow-up fashion which is more appropriate for the masses.
